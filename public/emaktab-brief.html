How it works

01 · LOGIN
Playwright signs in
Reads creds from .env.local, posts to login.emaktab.uz. Never logged, never committed. Captcha-aware.
02 · EXTRACT
Four scrapers
profile, grades (5 periods), diary (deep walk), homework (paginated). Each isolated and resilient.
03 · NORMALISE
One canonical pass
Subject names, mark types and IDs into one consistent shape. Pure functions, self-tested.
04 · SURFACE
Typed loader feeds the app
lib/emaktab.ts serves the snapshot to the sky, parent dashboard, and homework Socratic walkthrough.

The deep diary walk — what makes this hard

emaktab's /marks shows one week at a time. There's no URL date param. No <input type="date">. The only control is two arrow-like divs sitting as siblings of the day-cards container.

Login on login.emaktab.uz. Capture session cookies.
Land on emaktab.uz/marks, scrape the default week. Each day card carries data-test-id="day-{unixTs}" — the dateKey is exact.
Click [data-test-id="arrow-left"] programmatically (JS click bypasses ad overlays). emaktab swaps the day set via XHR; the URL stays the same.
Wait for the rendered day-{ts} id set to change AND for at least one day card to render — avoids scraping mid-transition.
Dedupe by dateKey (unix ts). When the visible window overlaps a previously-seen week we keep the smaller card (true day, not wrapper).
Stop when six clicks in a row don't move the earliest-visible-day backward — emaktab's actual data boundary. We hit it at 2025-09-02 — Day 1 of the school year.
