06 · What's ours scorpius · 2026

Not a model wrapper. The IP is ours.

01 · DSL
12 lesson card types
Discover, mcq, sequence, numberline, sort, diagram, ask, story — a typed grammar for Socratic teaching.
02 · Adapter
Model-agnostic LLM layer
One LanguageModel interface. Swap Gemini ↔ OpenAI with one env var. Future models swap in for free.
03 · RAG
Uzbek curriculum graph
Real textbooks ingested via gpt-5.1 vision — every topic resolved to its lesson. Not scraped from the web.
04 · Cache
SHA-keyed Firestore cache
Every expensive AI call (image, lesson, brief) is cached by content hash. One student pays — every student after is free.
