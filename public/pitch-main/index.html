04 · Solution

How Scorpius solves those problems.

01
Connect.Student authorises us with their own emaktab credentials. We fetch grades, schedule, and homework. Encrypted, for that user only — no scraping.
02
Personalize.Knowledge-tracing engine finds the missed topic and generates a personal 8-minute lesson — Socratic, in Uzbek, calibrated to the textbook the school actually uses.
03
Teach + Brief.Student swipes the lesson card-by-card; the star ignites. Parent gets a daily Uzbek-language brief: what was learned, what to ask at dinner.
