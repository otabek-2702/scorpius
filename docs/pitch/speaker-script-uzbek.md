## Q&A pack — top 8 questions + 30-sec answers

### Q1: "Why won't emaktab just build this themselves?"

> Ular qila olishi mumkin — lekin ular bunga **5 yil** ketadi. Biz **5 hafta** ichida MVP
> ko'rsatdik. Bizning maqsadimiz emaktab bilan **raqobat emas**, ular bilan **hamkorlik**.
> Biz darslik qatlami — ular tarmoq. Bugun ularga rasmiy xat yubordik (ko'rsating
> `docs/emaktab-outreach.md`).

### Q2: "How does the kundalik integration work? Can I try it with my account?"

> Bugun demo bizning oldindan keshlangan namuna ma'lumotidan oladi — 843 baho, 199 kun,
> bitta sinov akkaunti uchun to'liq pulldown qildik (`scripts/extract-emaktab.ts`). Bu
> infra **tayyor**. Buni har bir foydalanuvchi uchun real vaqtda ishlatish — **bir kunlik
> server ishi**. Final round'gacha tayyor qilamiz va sizning akkauntingizda jonli
> ko'rsatamiz.

### Q3: "What's your business model?"

> Freemium. **Bepul:** kuniga 3 ta dars. **To'lov:** 60 000 so'm/oy. Yana — **maktab
> litsenziyasi**: bitta maktab har bir o'quvchiga 20 000 so'm/oy. 100 maktab × 1 000
> o'quvchi = oyiga 2 milliard so'm.

### Q4: "Why Uzbek and not Russian?"

> O'zbek tilida sifatli o'quv kontenti **deyarli yo'q**. YouTube'da 80% kontent rus tilida.
> Bizning brand pozitsiyamiz: **o'zbek o'quvchisi o'z tilida o'qiydi**. Bu chuqur
> emotsional bog'lanish — ota-onalar pul to'laydi.

### Q5: "Won't Khan Academy / ChatGPT just replace you?"

> Khan Academy bizning audientsiya uchun rus/ingliz tilida. ChatGPT — generic chatbot.
> Bizda **uchta xususiyat ularda yo'q**: (1) **emaktab integratsiyasi** — ularda nol;
> (2) **O'zbek pedagogikasi va darsliklari**; (3) **ota-ona qatlami** — bu bizning eng
> kuchli moat.

### Q6: "How accurate is the AI? What if it teaches wrong?"

> Har bir dars **darslikka asoslangan** — biz o'zbek darsliklarini vision orqali raqamli
> qilganmiz va RAG'da ishlatamiz. AI generation darslikdan tashqariga chiqmaydi. Yana —
> har bir karta **tipi tekshirilgan** (mcq, sequence, etc.). Free-form javoblar uchun
> Sokratik usul: AI tushuntiradi, lekin "to'g'ri javob" sifatida darslik tan olinadi.

### Q7: "Where's the moat? Anyone can build this with GPT."

> Quyidagi to'rttasini o'sha dushman bir kechada qura olmaydi: (1) **Card DSL** — biz 36
> soatda qildik, sizga 6 oy; (2) **O'zbek curriculum RAG** — 12 ta darslikni vision
> orqali qilish — bir oylik mehnat; (3) **kesh qatlami** — har bir model chaqiruvi
> hash-keylangan — yangi foydalanuvchi har bir narsa bepul; (4) **emaktab partnership** —
> rasmiy xatimiz bo'lsa, **biz birinchimiz**.

### Q8: "What do you need from us?"

> Uchta narsa. **Birinchi:** waitlist'da bo'ling — QR'ni skanerlang, farzandingiz uchun
> sinab ko'ring. **Ikkinchi:** agar emaktab tomonidan bo'lsangiz — bizga 30 daqiqa vaqt
> bering. **Uchinchi:** seed bosqichida 50 000 dollar — 10 ta to'liq maktab pilot uchun.

## Voice + delivery notes

- **Pace:** 110–120 words/min. Slower than conversational. Each big number gets a beat.
- **Volume:** Project to the back row. The mic does the rest.
- **Eye contact:** rotate through 3 quadrants of the room. Never stare at one face.
- **Hands:** open palms when explaining the moat. Closed fist only on "**we** built this in 36 hours."
- **Posture:** weight on both feet, even. Don't pace. Plant.
- **Phone use:** only when showing scan or saying "watch the counter tick." Never to read notes.

Trust the deck. Trust your team. Pitch like you've already won.
